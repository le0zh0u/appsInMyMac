	**Link:** [Near Lock](http://nearlock.me/ "下载链接")
	

* Alfred 2

	**Link:** [Alfred 2](https://www.alfredapp.com/ "下载链接")
	[Alfred 2 破解版](http://www.macapp.so/alfred/ "下载链接")
	
	````

	````
